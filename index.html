What is The Lazio's Hotel, Cancellation Policy?

Any cancellation, postponement or partial cancellation should be advised to the management of the hotel in writing.

The cancellation charge for Food and Beverage will be 50% within 7 days of the event and 100% within 48 hours of the event.

Accommodation Cancellations and No-Shows.

Reservations that are cancelled or reduced in length of stay, or numbers, are subject to cancellation charges and no-show fees at the discretion of the Hotel and are as follows:

For any cancellation or reduction in number between 14 and 35 days from noon on the date of accommodation - 15% of the value of the booking.

For any cancellation or reduction in number between 14 and 2 days from noon on the date of accommodation - 33% of the value of the booking.

In case of cancellation within 48 hours from noon to the date of accommodation, or in case of no-show - 100% of the value of the booking.

What is The Lazio Hotel's Child Policy
For Child (ren) accompanying adult in same room: Age:
  • Upto 3 years sharing with adult/s – No Charge
  • Over 3 years up to 12 years sharing with 1 or 2 adults – Each child pays 50% of applicable per person adult double room rate.
  • Over 12 years up to 16 years sharing with 1 or 2 adults ‐ Each child pays 75% of applicable per person adult double room rate.
  • Up to 16 years having exclusive use of room – Room will be charged at 75% of applicable full adult single/double/triple room rate.
